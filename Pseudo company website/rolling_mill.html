ROLLING MILL

S.K. Samanta & Co.Pvt.Ltd is an infrastructural based company and handles turnkey EPC projects. This company diversified into "Steet-Sector" and setup an automated TMT at Bilaspur, Chattisgarh state.
The land area is 31.75 acres and is located in the industrial growth centre, Silpahari,Bilaspur. The capasity of this State-of-Art technology TMT bar mill is 150,000MT per annum and sizes are 8mm to 32mm.
It is a continuous mill and hence is more productive as well as pollution free plant as norms/standards of Pollution Control Board have been strictly followed and are being monitored. Plant is equipped with latest
digital testing machine to maintain quality.
